"Welcome to the real world."

This outdated idiom is one many of us recall not so fondly, a tired and overused phrase used tell young adults that "that's just the way life is." I promise, the "real world" is not as scary as this phrase makes it out to be. This site pools together advice from Cal Poly students who have been to the real world and made it back unscathed. Read on to discover their tips and tricks for surviving — dare I say, thriving — life post-grad. Wisdom awaits...
